ABOUT

My Story

I’m a meticulous and detail-driven web-QA-tester-turned-UX-Designer with a background in photography, customer service, and support. Testing has given me an immense appreciation for the user research aspect of UX and a desire to help prevent usability issues early in the project lifecycle. To the table, I bring the 25 years of expert Photoshop experience I’ve somehow managed to slip under my belt through both creative and web design. Well, one thing led to another and — TA-DAH! — my intermediate web development skills were born! And, because efficiency is important to me, “streamlining and optimising processes” has played a big role in the work I’ve done over the years.

My time in customer service and managing support put me on the frontline of the user experience and has made me more empathetic towards the humans on the receiving end of products and services.

From a young age, I’ve always wanted to help make life easier for people. Now, with an employer who knows what it means to make a difference in the world, I can put my extensive UX skill set to use doing just that!
