PROJECT

INK TANK

A tattoo inspiration and artist finder web app to minimize tattoo regret.
DATE AUG 18 – MAR 19 COMMISSIONED BY CareerFoundry (Course Project)
MY ROLE UX Architect, User Researcher, Visual Designer
NOTES Finished one week early despite delays in the wireframing process.

CASE STUDY OVERVIEW

Problem

Jason, a Tattoo Enthusiast, needs a way to search and preview designs, locate Artists near him, and feel supported in his search for the perfect artist/design combo so that he doesn’t have “buyer’s remorse”.

Solution

Architect a custom experience based on Jason's profile questions that offers up compatible Artists, custom content, a design editor, chat, and booking feature.

INTENDED OUTCOME: Positive reviews from Enthusiasts who report a bespoke experience and increased confidence in their decision. Tattoo Artists report an increase in and better relationships with clients.
CHALLENGES
ALONG THE WAY: 		Getting buy-in from Tattoo Artists in the beginning. None wanted to participate in interviews and, by extension, usability tests, so online research was done to fill in the gaps about Tattoo Artists and the focus was shifted to the Enthusiast’s user journeys for the rest of the exercise.
FUTURE PLANS: Continue with MVPs for the booking and editing journeys as well as flesh out the questionnaire. Once complete, use these to get buy-in from Tattoo Artists to further develop their journeys.
CONCLUSION: This is an app that both Tattoo Enthusiasts and Artists can get behind and enjoy using. Come for inspiration. Leave with no regrets.
